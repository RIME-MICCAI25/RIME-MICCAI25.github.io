Submission Guidelines

Our workshop will use the CMT online submission system: Submit via CMT.

Paper Formatting

Papers must follow the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) format, with a maximum of 8 pages for content (including figures and tables) and up to 2 additional pages for references. The file format for submission is PDF. Submissions that do not conform to the LNCS format or exceed the page limit will be rejected without review. The official Springer LNCS style files (LaTeX and Word) can be found on the Springer website.

Blind Review

Reviewing is double-blind. Authors must ensure that their identity is not revealed in the submitted manuscript. Please consult the MICCAI 2025 Anonymization Guidelines for full instructions.

Supplemental Material

Submission of supplemental material is optional. All supplemental content must be appropriately referenced within the main paper. Note that reviewers are not obligated to review supplemental material.

Simultaneous Submission

By submitting a manuscript, authors confirm that the work is original and not under review or accepted elsewhere. Papers with substantially similar content must not be submitted to another venue until our workshop decisions are released.

Proceedings

Accepted papers will be published in workshop proceedings, as part of the Springer LNCS series.
